Who is a Web Developer

A web developer is responsible for designing, building, and maintaining websites using programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They work closely with clients or stakeholders to understand their needs and develop solutions that meet their requirements. The job also involves ensuring that the website is user-friendly, easy to navigate, and visually appealing. Additionally, web developers are responsible for testing the website's performance, identifying and fixing bugs, and ensuring that the website is secure from potential threats. They may work independently or as part of a team, collaborating with other developers, designers, and project managers to complete projects on time and within budget. Overall, a web developer plays a crucial role in creating and maintaining an online presence for businesses and organizations.
